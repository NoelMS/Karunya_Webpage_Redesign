Testimonials

"I am Samyuktha S, an alumna of Karunya University in Robotics and Automation (2022). Currently, I work as a research engineer at Hyundai Mobis R&D. They provided unwavering support and abundant opportunities for growth, including involvement in organizations like IAESTE and IEEE, and founding CAIRA. Karunya has been pivotal in nurturing my potential and facilitating personal growth, making my college years profoundly rewarding. Here’s to Karunya University and its community!"

"Karunya has opened the door to enhance my skills in AI and robotics, which helped me secure a 9.76 CGPA in M.Tech Robotics and Automation. Currently, I am working as an ADAS Engineer at Renault Nissan Technology and Business Centre India, Chennai. The blend of academic excellence and practical experience has been instrumental in shaping my career and providing me with the skills needed to excel in this field."

- LAKSHMANAPRAKASH S B
